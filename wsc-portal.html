External Schedule Refresh
Pulls the conference Master Schedule Google Sheet (the same one the Xpression Hub reads) and upserts rows into wsc_external_events. Filtered to football, basketball, and (Sam Houston only) soccer/volleyball/baseball/softball, then merged with the ESPN broadcast schedule on Master + Manual tabs.
Encoder Availability
Deactivate an encoder when it's physically unavailable (offline, in repair, reserved). Inactive encoders are greyed out in every Manual Capture dropdown until you reactivate them. The 4-hour conflict indicator is automatic — encoders booked to another event whose start time is within 4 hours of the row are also disabled, with the conflicting matchup shown.
CUSA Encoder IPs
Update the Roku and HELO IP for each encoder when a device is replaced or its DHCP lease changes. Saved values overlay the static defaults baked into cusa_backend.py + wsc_data.json; the relay agent picks them up within ~10 seconds with no restart needed. Click Reset on a row to revert that encoder back to the file default.
